# Magus Generic Quest

Based on Shattered Star ch1-2. 5 levels.

## Concept

Magnimar, Varisia. Hunt fragments of the Sihedron (Thassilonian artifact). Descend through undercity, caves, ancient ruins.

Spell+sword fantasy - ancient magic items, arcane dungeons. All subterranean.

## Structure

### Level 1: Home
Get quest from Pathfinder Society (Magnimar lodge). Track down rogue agent Natalya Vancaskerkin.

### Level 2: Magnimar Undercity
Sewers and tunnels beneath the city.
- Wererats
- Sczarni (thieves)
- Derro dero? How the heck do you even spell it

### Level 3: Mushfens Caves
Tunnel network through the swamp.
- Boggards
- Troglodytes
- Swamp creatures

### Level 4: Lady's Light (depths)
Ancient Thassilonian lighthouse, lower levels.
- Gray Maidens (clone soldiers)
- Constructs
- Magical wards/traps

### Level 5: Sea Caves
Flooded caverns beneath the lighthouse.
- Aquatic creatures
- Clone vats
- Boss: ancient caretaker (Thassilonian guardian)
