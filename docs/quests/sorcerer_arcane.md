# Sorcerer Quest (Arcane Bloodlines)

Based on Return of the Runelords ch1-2. 5 levels.

like the other sorc stuff this is a bit scratchy need to dive a bit more into
the ap docs but thematically it's probably ok

## Concept

Varisia, Thassilonian ruins. Ancient sin magic.

Fits: arcane, destined, draconic, fey bloodlines.

## Structure

### Level 1: Home
Get quest. Travel to Roderic's Cove.

### Level 2: Roderic's Cove
Coastal town
- Cultists (Runelord revivalists)
- Animated objects
- Town undead

### Level 3: Churlwood
Haunted forest.
- Fey (corrupted)
- Forest creatures
- Thassilonian remnants

### Level 4: Hollow Mountain (upper)
Ancient Thassilonian ruin.
- Sinspawn
- Constructs
- Magical traps

### Level 5: Hollow Mountain (depths)
Runelord laboratory.
- Sinspawn varieties
- Bound outsiders
- Boss: Runelord cultist leader or awakened construct

## Notes

- Thassilonian runes, ancient artifacts
- Quest giver: local contact or Pathfinder Society
- Nemesis: Runelord cultist leader
