# Sorcerer Quest (Elemental Bloodlines)

Based on Legacy of Fire ch1-2. 5 levels.

## Concept

Qadira/Katapesh region. Genie-touched lands, elemental power.

Fits: fire, water, air, earth, genie bloodlines.

## Structure

### Level 1: Home
Get quest. Travel to Kelmarane.

### Level 2: Kelmarane (outskirts)
Ruined trading post, outer areas.
- Gnolls, hyenas
- Pugwampis (gremlins)
- Desert hazards

### Level 3: Kelmarane (town)
Clear the town proper.
- Gnoll warriors, flind
- Harpy
- Undead

### 4,5 House of the Beast
Gnoll temple of Rovagug.
- Desert travel
- Gnoll patrols
- Div (evil genie-kin)
- Gnoll priests
- Bound elementals
- Boss: gnoll high priest or bound div
