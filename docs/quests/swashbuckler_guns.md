# Swashbuckler Quest (Guns)

Based on Outlaws of Alkenstar ch1. 5 levels.

## Structure

### Level 1: Home

### Level 2: Alkenstar (slums)
- Street gangs
- Clockwork guards
- Thugs

### Level 3: Alkenstar (sewers/undercity)
- Criminals
- Vermin
- Corrupt shieldmarshals

### Level 4: Alkenstar (bank/vault)
- Security constructs
- Traps
- Guards

### Level 5: Alkenstar (upper city)
- Elite guards
- Clockwork enforcers
- Boss: the mogul
