# Monk Generic Quest

Based on Fists of Ruby Phoenix ch1-2. 5 levels.

## Concept

it's a bit of a phone it in but fuck it monks don't deserve more.

Tian Xia. Compete in the legendary Ruby Phoenix Tournament. Fight the world's greatest warriors to claim a prize from Hao Jin's vault.

Works for all focuses: punch, shoot, or ki-blast your way through.

## Structure

### Level 1: Danger Island
Pre-qualifier. Survive the island, prove worthy of entry.
- Rival teams
- Island hazards
- Beasts

### Level 2: Tournament Round 1
First elimination bouts.
- Diverse martial artists
- Monster competitors
- Arena hazards

### Level 3: Goka (city)
Move from one arena to another through city (r&c?)
- Urban encounters
- Rival competitors
- Assassins/cheaters
- primarily humanoid PC style monsters?

### Level 4: Tournament Round 2
Later elimination bouts, tougher opponents.
- Elite martial artists
- Magical fighters
- Dirty tricks (cheating? traps? triggers?)

### Level 5: Finals
Claim victory.
- Tournament champion
- Boss: reigning champion

## Notes

- Focus doesn't matter - all monks can compete
- Quest giver: Hao Jin (Ruby Phoenix) or tournament official
- Nemesis: THE CHAMPION
