# Kineticist Generic Quest

Based on Age of Ashes ch1-2. 5 levels.

## Concept

Isger/Cheliax border. Investigate fires at ruined Hellknight citadel, discover portal network, travel to elemental-attuned destination.

Key hook: portal destination changes based on player's element.

## Structure

### Level 1: Home
Get quest from Hellknight survivor. Travel to Breachill.

### Level 2: Hellknight Hill
Clear the ruined citadel, discover portal network.
- Cinderclaws (kobold cult)
- Charau-ka (demon ape-folk)
- Hellknight remnants (undead/constructs)
- Find Alseta's Ring (portal chamber)

### Level 3: Elemental Approach
Portal leads to element-appropriate location. Outer areas.
- Fire: volcanic region
- Water: underwater/coastal
- Earth: deep caverns
- Air: mountain peaks
- Element-appropriate creatures

### Level 4: Elemental Depths
Deeper into the elemental realm.
- Corrupted elementals
- Cult of Cinders outpost
- Elemental hazards

### Level 5: Cult Sanctum
Confront the Cult of Cinders leader in their elemental stronghold.
- Charau-ka high priest
- Bound elementals
- Boss: cult leader (fire-themed, adapts to player's element as opposition?)

## Notes

- Element choice matters - different level 3-5 aesthetics
- Portal network = excuse for elemental plane access
- Burn mechanic fits dangerous elemental environments
- Quest giver: Hellknight survivor
- Nemesis: Cult of Cinders leader (charau-ka high priest)
