# Fists of the Ruby Phoenix

Fists of the Ruby Phoenix, the twenty-ninth Pathfinder Adventure Path and the first Adventure Path to begin at a level higher than 1st level, released on July 7, 2021.

## Chapters

### Despair on Danger Island

Thirty-two teams of the world's greatest warriors, warlocks, and wanderers vie for their chance to compete in the Ruby Phoenix Tournament by surviving the perils of Danger Island in a first-of-its-kind prequalifying round. To earn entry to the legendary sorcerer Hao Jin's world-famous tournament, the contenders must fight ruthless rivals, endure harrowing hazards, and impress ever-watchful enforcers. The Fists of the Ruby Phoenix Adventure Path begins with "Despair on Danger Island," a complete adventure for 11th- to 14th-level characters.

### Ready? Fight!

The Ruby Phoenix Tournament officially begins! The contenders arrive in the legendary port city of Goka. Here, they'll give their all in a double-elimination competition to earn the title of Ruby Phoenix Champions and claim a prize from the legendary sorcerer Hao Jin's vault of wonders. ### King of the Mountain

When a devastating attack leaves the outcome of the Ruby Phoenix Tournament up in the air, the competitors must rise from the ashes and track down both the villains responsible for the disaster and the Ruby Phoenix herself. All signs point to the soaring mountain range around Goka, but only the gods know what challenges await the heroes atop the legendary Wall of Heaven. The Fists of the Ruby Phoenix Adventure Path concludes with "King of the Mountain," a complete adventure for 18th- to 20th-level characters.
