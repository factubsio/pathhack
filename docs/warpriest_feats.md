# Warpriest Class Feats

## Core Class Features (granted, not feats)

- Sacred Weapon (scaling damage with favored weapon)
- Blessings (chosen at level 1, cooldown-based)
- Spell slots (5 levels)
- Medium armor + favored weapon proficiency
- Fervor (TODO)

## Fervor

Cooldown-based ability (~60 turns), like blessings. Uses:
- Swift-cast any spell (costs fervor + slot)
- Quick self-heal (costs fervor only)
- Enhance weapon with property (flaming/holy/etc, duration)

Class feats can reduce cooldown, add charges, enhance effects.

## Existing Feats

- **Swift Blessing** - blessing is free action
- **Truly Blessed** - blessing cooldown -25%
- **Sacred Strike** - +2 attack with favored weapon

## Feat Ideas

### Blessing Enhancers
- **Extra Blessing** - pick another blessing
- **Blessing Focus** - blessing effects stronger/longer
- **Dual Blessing** - use two blessings at once?

### Fervor Enhancers
- **Fervent Healer** - fervor heal is stronger
- **Fervent Caster** - swift-cast at +1 CL
- **Sacred Armament** - weapon enhance lasts longer / more properties
- **Quickened Fervor** - reduced cooldown

### Sacred Weapon
- **Sacred Strike** (exists) - +2 attack
- **Sacred Critical** - bonus effect on crit with favored weapon
- **Weapon of Faith** - favored weapon counts as holy/silver/etc
