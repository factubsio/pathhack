# Monster Templates (1e)

## Simple +1 CR
- **Advanced** - generic stat buff
- **Giant** - bigger version
- **Fighter** (+1 or +2) - better combat stats
- **Barbarian** (+2 or +3) - rage, HP, damage

## Planar/Taint
- **Fiendish** (+0 or +1) - demonic, Lamashtu-touched
- **Celestial** (+0 or +1) - holy
- **Half-Fiend** (+1 to +3) - serious demonic

## Undead
- **Skeleton** (varies) - basic undead
- **Zombie** (varies) - tougher undead
- **Skeletal Champion** (+1) - smart skeleton
- **Zombie Lord** (+1) - smart zombie

## Weird
- **Fungoid** (+1) - fungal infection
- **Nightmare Creature** (+1) - Lamashtu nightmares
- **Fey-Touched** (+0 or +1) - fey influence

## 2e Simple
- **Elite** (+1 level) - tougher
- **Weak** (-1 level) - easier
